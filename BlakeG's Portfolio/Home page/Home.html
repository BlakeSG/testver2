    A breif history of me

    Hello, I'm Blake Gillanders, a 20-year-old who has spent the majority of my life in the picturesque town of Nelson, New Zealand. Welcome to my evolving portfolio. While it's still in its infancy, this website represents a significant milestone in my coding journey, serving as my inaugural project for Web504. Through this endeavor, I'm actively mastering HTML, CSS, and eagerly anticipating delving into JavaScript. Before I delve into my academic pursuits, let me share a bit about my background. I came into this world on August 29th, 2002, in the quaint town of Okiwi Bay, nestled within the serene Marlborough Sounds. Although my memories of those early days are hazy, my family relocated to Nelson just six months later, setting the stage for my formative years. Nelson Central School marked my first foray into the world of computers. My initial interactions involved video games, sparking an enduring fascination with technology . Fast forward to the age of 13, when my best friend embarked on building his first PC. Our collective lack of knowledge culminated in a functional but glitch-ridden result. Intrigued by the process, I saved diligently to construct my own PC a year later, this time with a deeper understanding. This endeavor ignited my genuine passion for computers, driving me to run a YouTube channel that flourished with 10,000 subscribers at its zenith. There, I honed skills in software like Sony Vegas Pro 13 and Adobe Photoshop CS6, forming the bedrock of my multimedia expertise. However, as adulthood beckoned, I took on a full-time job at a factory while grappling with my future aspirations. A year later, I met my fiancée, Pallas (pictured on the left at the top of this page). As our relationship blossomed, we embarked on a journey together, and I made the impromptu decision to part with my beloved setup. Six months later, I found myself yearning to reconnect with my tech roots. This journey led me to explore various career paths, including a stint as an engineer's assistant, where I delved into fabrication and welding. While instructive, it became evident that it wasn't my ultimate calling. Subsequently, I ventured into 'Van and a Man,' initially with hopes of securing a Class 2 license. After a few clashes with the boss, I returned to my previous job, contemplating my next move. It was during this period that I stumbled upon the 'Bachelor of IT' program at NMIT. Driven by curiosity, I delved into the world of coding, initially through platforms like 'freeCodeCamp' and 'Codecademy.' The allure of web development and software creation beckoned, and I took the plunge by investing in a powerful laptop. Shortly thereafter, I received acceptance into NMIT, cementing my commitment to the world of IT. I've embarked on a journey to acquire coding skills while meticulously crafting this website. On this site, you'll find four distinct sections. The 'Home' page, where you currently reside, offers this personal introduction. The 'My Skills' page showcases my qualifications, qualifications, and a downloadable copy of my CV. The 'Projects' page is where you can explore the diverse array of projects I've undertaken, available for download in HTML or PDF formats. Lastly, the 'Contact' page provides my contact information and a convenient form for any inquiries. Thank you for visiting my website. I hope you enjoy your stay, and please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or just want to connect. Cheers!
